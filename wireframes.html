Desktop User-flow: How to buy a product?

Step 1: Click the "SHOP" button on the left corner for a dropdown menu.

Step 2: Click the category that you are interested in from drop down menu, and use the filter to shop the proper products. Click the image of the product for details.

Step 3: Click your preference color and add this product into your cart.

Step 4: Check your cart and continue to check out.

Step 5: Fill in your information for shipping and finish your payment. You can also go back for shopping again at your confirmation page.

Phone User-flow: How to buy a product?

Step 1: Click the "SHOP" button on the left corner for a dropdown menu.

Step 2: Click the category that you are interested in from drop down menu, and use the filter to shop the proper products. Click the image of the product for details.

Step 3: Click your preference color and add this product into your cart.

Step 4: Check your cart and continue to check out if there's nothing you want to add.
